## 木人樁 — 詠春拳的最強工具

Almost synonymous with Wing Chun Kung Fu is the Muk Yan Jong or wooden man (a.k.a. the wooden dummy). Well known but often misunderstood, the Muk Yan Jong is considered Wing Chun's most powerful tool for developing true mastery of the art.

**Sifu Gary Lam**, an expert in the Wong Shun Leung Wing Chun lineage, describes the Wooden Man as essential for the refinement of a Wing Chun practitioner's advanced skills.

幾乎與詠春拳齊名的就是 **「木人樁」**。木人樁廣為人知卻常被誤解，它被認為是詠春拳培養真正大師級功力的最強大工具。黃淳樑系詠春專家 **「林文學師傅」** 形容，木人樁對於詠春練習者精進高階技巧至關重要。

---

## 木人樁訓練的真正目標

The goal of wooden man training is the goal of Wing Chun itself, that is to integrate and harmonize all of one's training into a coordinated and unfixed expression of the concepts and skills that define the form of Wing Chun Kung Fu.

木人樁訓練的目標即是詠春拳本身的目標，也就是將個人所有的訓練整合並協調成一種靈活的表達方式，展現定義詠春拳的理念與技巧。

> Sigung Wong would often say that it is a mistake to be controlled by Wing Chun, that Wing Chun was only a name and not some kind of dogma.

> 黃師公常說，被詠春拳控制是一個錯誤，詠春只是一個名稱，而不是某種教條。

This point is paramount to the true mastery of Wing Chun, to not see it as a static and rigid approach to martial arts, but rather as a form for allowing us to develop and explore the concepts and skills that are common to all of the highest expressions of martial arts.

這點對於真正掌握詠春拳至關重要：不要將其視為死板、僵化的武術方法，而是將其視為一種形式，讓我們得以發展和探索所有最高境界武術所共有的理念與技巧。

> The wooden man training is one of the essential steps to understanding and achieving this lofty goal, of going beyond the outward appearance and form of Wing Chun and instead **freely working with its fundamental nature in a spontaneous and undetermined way**.

> 木人樁訓練是理解並達成這個崇高目標的重要步驟之一，讓我們能超越詠春拳的外表與形式，進而以**自發且不受限的方式，自由運用其基本本質**。

## 木人樁的構造

The Muk Yan Jong's construction presents:

| 部位 | Component | 數量 |
|------|-----------|------|
| **手臂** | Arms | 3 個位置 |
| **腿部** | Leg | 1 個位置 |
| **主幹** | Center trunk | 代表對手身體 |

It is traditionally mounted on a wall or implanted in the ground. This provides a stable three-dimensional training surface, allowing for the practice of movements against the inside and outside angles of an opponent's body.

木人樁的結構提供了三個手臂位置、一個腿部位置，當然還有代表對手身體的中央主幹。傳統上它是安裝在牆上或固定在地面的。這提供了一個穩定的三維立體訓練表面，讓練習者能針對對手身體的內外角度練習動作。

### 從 108 到 120 — 詠春不是靜態武術

The traditional form has 108 separate movements; this has been expanded upon, and now boasts 120 techniques.

| | 傳統 Traditional | 現行 Current |
|---|---|---|
| **動作數量** | 108 | 120 |

Changes like this take place because Wing Chun is not a static martial art, it is always seeking to improve and develop itself, and innovations have occurred. Innovations such as these come from a teacher's experience in application.

發生這樣的改變是因為詠春不是一門靜態的武術，它始終在尋求自我改進與發展，並且不斷創新。這類創新來自於老師在實際應用中的經驗。

> For example, Sifu Lam's teacher **Wong Shun Leung** made certain changes to Wing Chun, with Grandmaster **Yip Man's** approval, that were based on years of winning challenge matches (beimo).

> 例如，林師傅的老師**黃淳樑**在**葉問**宗師的批准下，對詠春拳做出了某些改變，這些改變是基於多年來在挑戰賽（比武，Beimo）中獲勝的經驗。

## 四大特質 — 掌握詠春的關鍵

Sifu Lam denotes four essential qualities needed for the mastery of Wing Chun techniques and application. He sees these four key ideas as the common denominator in any type of mastery, not only in the practice of martial arts.

林師傅指出了掌握詠春技巧與應用所需的 **「四個基本特質」**。林師傅認為，這四個關鍵概念是任何領域達到大師境界的共同點，而不僅僅限於武術練習。

| 特質 | Quality | 說明 |
|------|---------|------|
| **準（Jun）** | Precision | 精確打擊目標 |
| **穩（Wan）** | Stability | 穩定的結構與根基 |
| **快（Fai）** | Speed | 動作的速度 |
| **勁（Geng）** | Power | 打擊的力量 |

> Siheng Mitch Grimm, a 220 pound body builder and Sifu Lam's senior student, when asked by a peer what kind of Wing Chun he was learning, he responded **"scary Wing Chun."**

> 林師傅的資深學生、體重 220 磅的健美運動員 Mitch Grimm 師兄，當被同儕問到他正在學習什麼樣的詠春時，他回答說：**「可怕的詠春。」**

Wing Chun at this level is a product of these four qualities of mastery working in unison, as one seamless action.

這個層次的詠春正是這四種大師特質協同運作、完美融合為一的產物。

### 隱藏的第五特質 — 殘忍

A hidden fifth quality is sometimes spoken about and that is **cruelty**. This fifth quality is not a physical attribute per se but instead is an attitude necessary for combat, a commitment required for survival.

有時人們會談到隱藏的第五種特質，那就是 **「殘忍」**。這第五種特質本身並不是身體屬性，而是一種戰鬥必備的態度，一種為了生存所需的決心。

To reach the highest levels of ability and someday become a graduate of the Gary Lam Wing Chun Kung Fu program, the student must make these qualities his very own. The Muk Yan Jong provides a controlled, deliberate opportunity to enhance and bring together these four qualities of mastery.

為了達到最高水準的能力，並有朝一日從林文學詠春拳課程中畢業，學生必須將這些特質內化為自己的一部分。木人樁提供了一個受控、刻意的機會來增強和融合這四種大師特質。

## 木人樁套路 — 三段訓練

The form itself consists of 120 movements broken into three distinct sections of training.

包含 120 個動作的套路分為 **「三個不同的訓練階段」**：

### 第一段 — 基本技巧與結構對齊

The first section is devoted to the basic techniques and structural alignment of Wing Chun.

第一部分專注於詠春的 **「基本技巧與身體結構對齊」**。

By this point in a student's training many of these movements have already been seen in the solo form training (i.e. Siu Nim Tao, Chum Kiu, and Biu Jee), what has changed is the student's opportunity now to train position, timing and his body's structural alignment.

到了這個訓練階段，許多動作已經在單人套路（即小念頭、尋橋和鏢指）中見過，現在改變的是，學生有機會訓練 **「位置、時機」** 以及 **「身體的結構對齊」**。

> Sifu Lam often says that a Wing Chun fighter **wins with his structure and angle and not with brute force**; otherwise there would be no point to developing martial ability.

> 林師傅常說，詠春拳手是靠 **「結構和角度」** 獲勝，而不是靠蠻力；否則練習武術就沒有意義了。

### 第二段 — 機會動作（Chance Actions）

The second section focuses on chance actions. These techniques involve different types of pushing and pulling actions, used to destabilize and disrupt an opponent's balance and structure.

第二部分側重於 **「機會動作」**。這些技巧涉及不同類型的推拉動作，用於破壞和干擾對手的平衡與結構。

These movements are usually done only when the opportunity presents itself, used instinctively as the opponent is in motion. Sifu Lam calls these types of techniques **helping actions**, used typically as a second movement to a primary hand.

這些動作通常只有在機會出現時才會使用，在對手移動時本能地施展。林師傅將這類技巧稱為 **「輔助動作」**，通常作為主要手法的第二動作。

| 動作類型 | 說明 |
|----------|------|
| **主要手法** | 直接打擊對手要害 |
| **輔助動作** | 協助主要手法，或將對手摔向環境障礙物 |

### 第三段 — 步法訓練

The third section is devoted exclusively to training the footwork.

第三部分專門用於訓練 **「步法」**。

Here the techniques of stepping, kicking, trapping, and leg brakes are honed. The techniques involve a combined use of the hands working in concert with the footwork applications.

這裡磨練了踏步、踢擊、封鎖和斷腿技巧。這些技巧涉及手部與步法應用的結合：

| 技巧 | Technique | 攻擊目標 |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **踏步** | Stepping | 佔據位置 |
| **踢擊** | Kicking | 腹部、膝蓋、腳踝 |
| **封鎖** | Trapping | 控制對手 |
| **斷腿** | Leg brakes | 膝蓋、腳踝關節 |

> This develops powerfully dominating actions that pull, trap and seize while at the same time attacking the stomach, knee and ankle joints.

> 發展出強大的壓制動作，能夠在拉扯、封鎖和控制對手的同時，攻擊胃部、膝蓋和腳踝關節。

### 三段訓練總覽

| 段落 | 重點 | 訓練目標 |
|------|------|----------|
| **第一段** | 基本技巧與結構 | 位置、時機、結構對齊 |
| **第二段** | 機會動作 | 推拉、破壞平衡、輔助動作 |
| **第三段** | 步法 | 踢擊、封鎖、斷腿 |

## 第二個教練 — Your Second Sifu

Sifu Lam calls the wooden dummy **your second coach** (or second Sifu).

林師傅將木人樁稱為你的 **「第二個教練」**（或第二個師傅）。

This is due to the fact that as you train on the dummy it will:

- **Correct your actions** — 糾正你的動作
- **Correct your position** — 糾正你的位置
- **Help develop power** — 幫助你發展力量
- **Train your timing** — 訓練你的時機

### 心意合一 — Sam Yi Hap Yat

Practicing at his full potential the student should be thinking about what the dummy is doing, visualizing himself in an actual confrontation. He should unite his actions with his intent and endeavor to harness his emotional power (**Sam Yi Hap Yat**).

在發揮最大潛力練習時，學生應該思考木人樁正在做什麼，想像自己處於實際的對抗中。他應該將自己的動作與意圖結合，並努力掌控自己的情感力量（**「心意合一」**）。

### 陰陽手 — Hard and Soft Actions

The student trains hard and soft actions (yin and yang hands), this often depending on when and why his limbs lose connection with the dummy.

學生訓練剛與柔的動作（陰陽手），這通常取決於他的四肢何時以及為何與木人樁失去接觸。

| 力量類型 | Power Type | 說明 |
|----------|-----------|------|
| **陽中有陰** | Yin within Yang | 柔中帶緊的打擊力量 |
| **陰中有陽** | Yang within Yin | 緊中帶柔的打擊力量 |

He develops an ability to apply soft and tight striking power and how not to let that power pass over and therefore miss his target.

他發展出施展柔與緊打擊力量的能力，並學會不讓力量流失而錯過目標。

### 彈性動作 — Springing Movement

> Crucial to proper training is creating a **springing movement** in the dummy, by matching the footwork with that movement the student uses the dummy's recoil to train timing and position.

> 正確訓練的關鍵是在木人樁中創造一種 **「彈性動作」**，透過步法配合這種動作，學生利用木人樁的後座力來訓練時機和位置。

## 追中線，不追手 — Chase The Centerline

The wooden man is essential to developing tight focused power and to closing the gates to your own vulnerabilities, while at the same time seeking the opponent's gaps no matter how protected they are.

木人樁對於發展緊密集中的力量以及關閉自身弱點的「門戶」至關重要，同時無論對手防禦多麼嚴密，也要尋找其破綻。

> Wong Shun Leung often said, **"Never chase your enemy's hands, always chase their centerline."** The exception to this rule is if they are armed.

> 黃淳樑常說：**「永遠不要追逐敵人的手，要始終追擊他們的中線。」** 唯一的例外是當他們持有武器時。

### 黃師公的教訓

Once in Sigung Wong's school a student during training had broken an arm from a Muk Yan Jong, thinking Wong would be pleased he presented the broken arm ready to be praised.

曾經在黃師公的武館裡，一名學生在訓練時打斷了一根木樁的手臂，他以為黃師公會很高興，便拿著斷臂準備受讚。

> Instead of being praised he was severely chastised. Sigung Wong scolded him in no uncertain terms, not only had he broken a perfectly good dummy arm but he was also **chasing hand and not the centerline**.

> 然而他不但沒有受到讚賞，反而受到了嚴厲的訓斥。黃師公毫不含糊地責罵他，他不僅打壞了一根完好的木樁手臂，而且他是在 **「追手」，而不是追中線」**。

> This goes to show that Muk Yan Jong training is very specific and capitalizes on the sound principles of Wing Chun open hand concepts for training. **The wooden dummy trains students on how to fight smarter, not harder.**

> 這說明木人樁訓練非常具體，並且充分利用了詠春拳開手概念的合理原則進行訓練。**「木人樁訓練學生如何更聰明地戰鬥，而不是更費力地戰鬥。」**

## 打空樁 — Training Away From The Dummy

Finally students train Muk Yan Jong by practicing away from the dummy, done by going through the movements in the air. This training perfects control in the form and helps to point out where any obvious mistakes or lapses are.

最後，學生會透過遠離木人樁進行練習，也就是在空氣中演練這些動作（**「打空樁」**）。這種訓練完善了套路中的控制力，有助於指出任何明顯的錯誤或疏漏。

> Wooden dummy training ultimately becomes a challenge match with a **shadow or thinking enemy**. The training routine is imagined as a real encounter, the practitioner sees each move as a response to an enemy's attack; this deeply impresses perfect control of the actions and their automatic application in combat.

> 木人樁訓練最終成為一場與 **「影子或假想敵的挑戰賽」**。訓練常規被想像成真實的遭遇戰，練習者將每一個動作視為對敵人攻擊的反應；這深深地印證了對動作的完美控制以及它們在戰鬥中的本能應用。

## 結語 — 精密工具，不是沙包

> The Muk Yan Jong **is not a piece of wood to toughen the students arms and legs on**, rather when used properly is a sophisticated tool for developing and mastering the most advanced skills in Wing Chun.

> 木人樁 **「不是一塊用來讓學生鍛鍊四肢使其堅硬的木頭」**，相反地，如果使用得當，它是發展和掌握詠春拳最高階技巧的精密工具。

It has a unique and profound place in martial arts training, being an essential study in the training of the best Wing Chun fighters for hundreds of years.

它在武術訓練中擁有獨特而深遠的地位，數百年來一直是培養最優秀詠春拳手不可或缺的學習項目。

## 傳承延續

Today teachers like **Sifu Gary Lam** carry on this important martial arts tradition, developing the next generation of practitioners and teachers.

如今，像 **「林文學師傅」** 這樣的導師傳承了這項重要的武術傳統，培養著下一代的練習者與教師。

Wing Chun's roots are as a martial technology of revolution and military political solutions, today it continues to flourish and grow for the benefit of society and the development of individual character.

詠春拳的根源是作為一種革命與軍事政治解決方案的武術技術，今天它為了社會的利益與個人品格的發展而繼續繁榮成長。

> Sifu Lam teaches at his training institute in Monterey Park, California. He is one of a handful of Sifus authorized to teach the complete Wong Shun Leung system.

> 林師傅在加州蒙特利公園市的訓練學院授課，傳承詠春拳傳統。他是少數獲授權教授完整黃淳樑系統的師傅之一。
