## The Roundhouse Kick

The roundhouse kick is one of the most powerful
techniques in martial arts.

### Steps

1. **Start in fighting stance**
2. **Pivot on your base foot** — turn it 90 degrees
3. **Rotate your hips** — power comes from here
4. **Extend the leg in an arc**
5. **Strike with the shin or instep**
6. **Retract quickly** — return to stance

### Common Mistakes

- Not pivoting the base foot
- Using only leg power (no hip rotation)
- Dropping your guard
- Leaning too far back

### Training Drill

Practice slow motion kicks:
- 10 kicks each leg
- Focus on form
- Gradually increase speed

> "The roundhouse kick is the king of kicks."
