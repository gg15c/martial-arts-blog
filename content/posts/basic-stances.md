## Why Stances Matter

Your stance is your foundation. Without a solid base,
no technique will be effective.

## Key Stances

### 1. Front Stance (Zenkutsu-dachi)
- Front knee bent over the toes
- Back leg straight
- Weight distribution: 60/40

### 2. Horse Stance (Kiba-dachi)
- Feet wide, parallel
- Knees bent outward
- Weight distribution: 50/50

### 3. Cat Stance (Neko-ashi-dachi)
- Most weight on the back leg (90/10)
- Front foot light and ready to kick

## Training Tip

Hold each stance for **60 seconds**.
Build up to **3 minutes** over time.
