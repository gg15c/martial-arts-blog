## The Front Kick

The front kick (mae geri) is one of the most fundamental
techniques across many martial arts styles.

### Key Points

1. **Chamber the knee** — Lift your knee high
2. **Extend the leg** — Snap the kick out
3. **Strike with the ball of the foot**
4. **Retract quickly** — Bring the leg back fast

### Common Mistakes

- Dropping your guard while kicking
- Leaning too far back
- Not chambering the knee high enough

> "A kick without proper form is just a leg swing."

### Training Drill

Repeat **3 sets of 10 kicks** on each leg daily
for one week. Focus on form over speed.
