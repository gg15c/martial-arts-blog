## 詠春不只是「打」

Wing Chun Kung Fu is best known for its powerful, relentless linear attacks. Seeking the path of least resistance, and then repeatedly striking to specific targets on the opponent's centerline. Wing Chun strives to end a conflict as decisively as possible, taking the most direct route available.

詠春拳最為人熟知的是它那種有力且不斷的直線攻擊。它會先找出最省力、最直接的路徑，然後針對對手身體正中線的特定部位反覆出拳。詠春的目標是盡快、果斷地結束衝突，走最直接的路。

Surprising to many is the fact that Wing Chun consists of several systems of training, of which the striking techniques are but only one part. Two of the arts other styles of training are the pushing and pulling systems of Wing Chun usage.

很多人以為詠春只有「打」的技巧，事實上它其實包括好幾套訓練系統，而「打」只是一部份。另一兩大訓練領域是**推與拉**的技巧，這是詠春裡的控制系統。

### 推與拉的用途

These techniques of control are used to manipulate, weaken, destabilize and project the opponent; usually used to assist the primary attacking techniques.

| 用途 | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **操弄** | Manipulate |
| **削弱** | Weaken |
| **使對手失去平衡** | Destabilize |
| **把對手推出去** | Project the opponent |

這些控制技巧用來操弄、削弱、使對手失去平衡，甚至把對手推出去；通常是配合主要的攻擊動作一起使用。

### 機會動作 — Chance Actions

These techniques are part of a class of fighting movements called **chance actions**, so called because they are only used when an opportunity or chance presents itself.

這類「推」與「拉」的動作屬於**機會動作**，意思是只有在出現適當機會時才會使用。

Pushing and pulling are developed to work with striking (crossing hand), closing (standing grappling) and footwork (kicking, traps, pushing, throwing and leg breaks) techniques, which when combined present the unified Wing Chun open hand system as was taught to Sifu Gary Lam (Lam Man Hog) by the late Sigung Wong Shun Leung.

它們會和以下三類技法協同運作：

| 類別 | Category | 包含技法 |
|------|----------|----------|
| **過手** | Crossing hand | 打擊技術 |
| **擒拿** | Standing grappling | 近身控制 |
| **步法** | Footwork | 踢、卡位、推、拋、踢斷腿等 |

當此三種技法一起運用時，就形成了詠春所說的**手法系統**。這套系統是已故黃淳樑師父傳授給林文學師父的完整教法。

## 林師父的四階段訓練體系

Sifu Lam presents the entirety of his Wing Chun system in four general levels; these different levels categorize the ability of each student and outline the curriculum of Sifu Lam's system in four stages of development.

林文學師父把他的詠春拳完整體系分成**四個大階段**，每個階段依學員的能力層級劃分，構成課程的發展藍圖。

### 第一層級 — 基礎與過手

Each student starts with a course of basic training, learning the fundamental concepts, principles and rudimentary applications of Wing Chun. Then the crossing hand system is taught; this covers all skills and techniques for going into an opponent's position and landing a combination of blows to the head and neck. In Sifu Lam's school this covers all training up to a particular technique called changing hand, this completes the material covered for level one.

每位學員一開始都會接受基礎課程，學習詠春的基本概念、原理以及最基礎的應用方式。接著會教授**過手系統**，這套技法教會學員如何切入對手的身形，並在對手的頭部與頸部同時出連續的拳擊。在林師父的教學裡，練到能完成叫**轉手**的動作，就代表第一層級的所有內容已經學完。

### 第二層級 — 機會動作、擒拿與步法

After level one has been properly digested the student enters into the second level training, focusing now on the chance action techniques as well as the closing and footwork training.

第一層級徹底消化之後，學員會進入第二層級的訓練，會聚焦在**機會行為的技巧**訓練，並同時加強**擒拿與步法**的練習。

| 層級 | Level | 訓練重點 |
|------|-------|----------|
| **第一層級** | Level 1 | 基礎概念、過手系統、轉手 |
| **第二層級** | Level 2 | 機會動作、擒拿、步法 |
| **第三層級** | Level 3 | 進階訓練 |
| **第四層級** | Level 4 | 大師級訓練 |

## 第二層級的黐手 — 從「打」到「控制」

The second level training emphasizes a different approach to skill development and especially a different way of doing Chi Sao (sticky hands).

第二層級的訓練方式跟第一層級大不相同，尤其是在黐手的練法上更是另類。

### 第一層級 vs 第二層級的黐手

When learning in the first level training, practicing Chi Sao is about hitting the opponent and trying to not get hit. In second level training the student starts to develop abilities to control the opponents fighting structure and also begins to develop how to fight with what is referred to as feeling, called by Sifu Lam "Natural reaction fighting".

| | 第一層級 | 第二層級 |
|---|---------|---------|
| **黐手目的** | 打到對手、不被打到 | 控制對手的結構 |
| **格鬥方式** | 主動攻擊 | 以感覺為主的「自然反應」 |
| **重點** | 打 | 控制 |

- **第一層級**練黐手時，重點是「打到」對手，同時盡量「不被打到」。
- 進入**第二層級**後，學員開始學會**控制對手的結構**，並逐步培養一種以感覺為主的格鬥方式，林師父稱之為**「自然反應」**。

### 自然反應格鬥

What this implies is that if you can control the opponent and react based on instinct, then you can do what you want, when you want and how you want.

這種「自然反應」的概念是：只要你能掌握對手的動作，並憑本能即時回應，就能在自己想要的時間、方式、方向上施展手法。

Training to control the opponent is significantly different than trying to hit only, it means disciplining yourself to not always hit and not being afraid of being hit. It means beginning to train chance techniques such as pushing and pulling, and of trying to feel what the opponent is doing and how they are doing it.

要做到這點，訓練的重點不再是「只會打」，而是，不一定要先出拳，但也不怕被對手擊中。因此，學員會開始練習**機會行為**，例如**推、拉**，同時學會感受對手的意圖與動作方式，藉此在實戰中以感覺而非單純力量取勝。

## 黐手的「玩」— 學會控制的藝術

Chi Sao training of this nature is often referred to as playing, learning to control instead of always trying to beat the opponent, it is definitely not free fighting. All out fighting would not allow for the opportunity needed to develop these skills.

這種黐手練法目的是學會控制，而不是一直想「打贏」對手。它絕對不是自由搏鬥；如果把所有能量都用來全力進攻，就根本沒有時間與機會去練習這些控制技法。

### 信任的重要性

This is an important distinction that must be made, because here you must trust and be trusted by your training partner. If you cannot train control because you are obsessed with hitting and not being hit by the opponent, then you will not have a training opportunity for advancement to the higher levels of Wing Chun. This is easy to understand but very difficult to do.

這裡有一個很重要的區別：**必須互相信任**，讓你的練習夥伴願意把自己交給你。如果你執著於「只想打中」而不敢被打，根本做不到控制的訓練，也就失去了升到詠春更高層次的機會。概念上很簡單，真正做到卻非常困難。

### 以開放、寬大的心練習

In practice this means being able to have the discipline, self-control and character to not always strike to win every match of Chi Sao, as Sifu Lam says, **"You must train with a open and big heart"**.

在實際練習時，意味著你必須有**紀律、克制與品格**，不把每一次黐手都當成必須全力擊中才能贏的比賽。正如林師父所說：**「要以開放、寬大的心來練習。」**

### 關掉肌肉力量

The student must in the end learn to turn off his muscle power; striving rather to develop subtle technique, keen sensitivity, and how to harness the body's innate structural power.

最終，學員需要學會**減少肌肉的力量**，改以**細膩的技巧、敏銳的感知**，以及運用身體固有的結構力量來取勝。

> It has been said of Grandmaster Yip Man that when he practiced Chi Sao he would only use control techniques, demonstrating in word and in deed his mastery of Wing Chun's highest levels.

> 據說，**葉問大師**練黐手時只使用控制技巧，展現了他對詠春最高層次的精通。

## 黐手中的核心原則

Chi Sao offers a training environment to develop these higher-level chance action skills such as pushing and pulling, which could not reasonably be developed under real life combat conditions.

黐手是一種專門用來培養高階**機會動作**（例如推、拉）的訓練方式，這類技巧在實戰中很難自然形成。

> Ultimately, if the student cannot overcome himself, then he has no chance of overcoming his opponent and truly learning the control side of Wing Chun kung fu.

> 最終，如果學員連自己都克服不了，就不會有機會克服對手，也無法真正領會詠春「控制」的一面。

### 必須維持的要素

All of this of course implies that the correct structure, sitting, facing, footwork and other core Wing Chun fighting concepts are maintained in Chi Sao, this is also usually very difficult.

要做到這點，黐手裡必須同時保持正確的核心概念：

| 要素 | Element | 說明 |
|------|---------|------|
| **結構** | Structure | 正確的身體架構 |
| **坐馬** | Sitting | 下盤穩固 |
| **朝形** | Facing | 正對對手 |
| **步法** | Footwork | 腳步移動 |

Difficult not only to physically maintain, but also emotionally difficult to be self-restrained.

這往往非常困難：

- **身體上**要持續維持正確姿勢不易
- **情緒上**要學會自我克制、不要因為想要「打」而失控

### 想快就慢走

The student wishing to progress must often slow down his techniques so that correct footwork and body structure are not lost to speed and an uncontrolled desire for making the hit.

想要進步的學員常常需要**放慢動作**，讓腳步與身體結構不因速度或衝動的「想擊中」而變形。

> Sifu Lam can often be heard saying, **"If you want to go faster, slow down, if you want to be stronger, train softer"**.

> 林師父常說的話正是這個道理：**「想跑得快就慢走，想變得強就練得柔。」**

這句話提醒我們，真正的力量來自於穩固的結構與柔和的控制，而不是單純的速度或硬碰硬的力量。

## 拉的訓練 — Pulling Training

Pulling training begins with learning the basic drills, these cover using an outside and inside arm pull, as well as an outside arm bar pull.

拉的練習先從基礎動作開始，內容包括：

| 技法 | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| **外門拉** | Outside arm pull |
| **內門拉** | Inside arm pull |
| **外門十字固定** | Outside arm bar pull |

### 拉的應用時機

These techniques are trained as both initial and following actions on the opponents leading or secondary attacking strikes, and serve to throw the attacker into environmental obstructions, to the ground or into another opponent.

這些拉的技巧既可以作為**先發制人的動作**，也可以作為**跟進的動作**，配合對手的第一波或第二波攻擊。目的在於把進攻者拋向四周的障礙物、地面，或是推向另一個對手，讓他失去平衡。

### 拉的戰術效果

Pulling also serves to disturb, destabilize and disrupt and opponents correct body structure, balance, direction and main attack.

拉還能擾亂、破壞對手的：

| 目標 | Target |
|------|--------|
| **身體結構** | Body structure |
| **平衡** | Balance |
| **方向** | Direction |
| **主要攻擊** | Main attack |

### 拉與推的配合

Pulling and pushing are often combined to work together, changing direction and throwing off the opponent's natural reaction to recover his balance. Chance actions are typically followed by footwork techniques such as a leg break or a throw, and crossing hand strikes such as Lap Sao and Pak Sao.

拉與推常常**結合使用**，互相配合改變對手的方向，打亂對手恢復平衡的反應。完成一次拉（或推）之後，通常會接**腳步技巧**，例如：

- 踢腳
- 拋摔

同時也會接**過手的手法**，如拉手、拍手，形成完整的「機會行為」。

## 推的訓練 — Pushing Training

Pushing training is separated into two categories, those being one handed or two-handed pushing techniques.

推的練習分成**兩大類**：

| 類別 | Category |
|------|----------|
| **單手推（Toi Sao）** | One handed pushing |
| **雙手推（Po Pai / 抱排）** | Two-handed pushing |

### 單手推（Toi Sao）

One handed pushing (toi sao) uses the lead hand to push on the opponent's centerline, usually applying pressure just below the shoulder at the humeral joint. The other hand is used to misdirect the opponent's position and to bring them slightly off balance, Sifu Lam calls this **making the opponent wrong**.

單手推的做法是，用前手在對手的中線上施壓，通常把力量放在肩膀以下、肱骨關節的部位。同時，後手則用來**誤導對手的身形**，把對手稍微弄偏、讓他失去平衡。林師父把這種動作稱作**「讓對手錯位」**。

### 讓對手錯位 — Making The Opponent Wrong

The idea of two or more movements for every one fighting action, such as initially making the opponent wrong just prior to performing the push, is a subtle but prevalent idea in Wing Chun. It is the kind of information that is hard to see and usually must be pointed out by one's teacher.

這裡的概念是：每一個格鬥動作，往往要配合**兩個或更多的動作**。例如，在真正推出去之前，先用後手「讓對手錯」——先把對手的重心或姿勢弄錯，再執行推的動作。這種多層次、先誤導後攻擊的技巧在詠春裡非常常見，但往往**不易被肉眼察覺**，需要師父在練習時特別指出。

> The concept of leading in Aikido or off balance (kuzushi) in Judo is very similar to Wing Chun's concept of making the opponent wrong.

> 這個概念與**合氣道的引導**或**柔道的崩**（崩し，讓對手失衡）非常相似，都是先把對手的平衡弄亂，再完成主要的攻擊。

### 雙手推 — 抱排（Po Pai / Butterfly Hands）

Two handed pushing (Po Pai or butter fly hands) is a more complex technique and is trained from eight different lead in hands, examples of which would be Bon Sao, Gan Sao, Hun Sao and so on.

雙手推是比較複雜的技巧，練習時會從**八種不同的抱排手**開始，常見的有膀、耕、圈等等。

### 抱排 vs 單手推

Also different about the Po Pai is that instead of ejecting the opponent away from you with the arms held straight as in Toi Sao (one handed pushing), Po Pai pushing follows the opponent at a close range for the entire push. This is done to maximize the power (taking the opponents position) of the push and to also protect the Wing Chun practitioner from being hit by a counter strike in the medium range.

| | 單手推（Toi Sao） | 抱排（Po Pai） |
|---|---|---|
| **手臂** | 伸直推出 | 貼近對手全程跟隨 |
| **距離** | 把對手推開 | 貼身推進 |
| **力量** | 一般 | 最大化（吃掉對手位置） |
| **防護** | 中距離有空隙 | 避免中距離反擊 |

與單手推把手臂伸直、直接把對手拋開不同，抱排的推法是在**貼近對手的距離**，整個推的過程都跟隨對手的身形。這樣做的目的是把力量最大化（把對手的位子吃掉），同時也能保護詠春練習者**避免被中距離的反擊擊中**。

### 抱排的雙重功能 — 推與打

Po Pai not only serves to push, but also to strike. Using internal power, a yin style iron palm strike is delivered to the opponent's internal organs along with the push.

抱排不只能推，還能同時**打擊**對方。使用內勁的掌，在推的同時把掌擊向對手的內臟。

Po pai is an action that must be executed with impeccable timing to be effective and relies heavily on the practitioner's development and use of structural power.

這一動作必須掌握**極佳的時機**才能發揮效用，且非常依賴練習者的**結構力量**（身體骨架與重心的運用）。

Po Pai can be used to project the opponent outside the fighting distance, and like the Toi Sao can be used to strike the opponent against objects in the environment including other opponents.

抱排可以把對手推出戰鬥距離，就像單手推一樣，也能把對手撞向環境中的物體（牆壁、柱子，甚至其他人），形成額外的破壞效果。

## 追手禁令 — 黃淳樑師公最重要的教條

Pulling and pushing as well as all other chance or secondary actions must be used with caution and never thought of as primary attacks.

拉、推以及所有機會行動都必須謹慎使用，絕不能把它們當成主要的攻擊手段。

### 追手的危險

The chief reason for this is what's called **chasing hands**, the prohibitions against which was one of Sigung Wong Shun Leung's most important edicts.

這樣規定的最主要原因是詠春裡的**追手禁令**——黃淳樑師公最重要的教條之一。

His conviction was that your principle goal should always be to attack directly to the enemy's centerline and therefore the most important targets. Any divergence from this philosophy was nothing short of opening your own defensive gaps and becoming a target yourself instead of attacking one.

| 原則 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **根本目標** | 永遠直接攻向對手的中線（頭、頸） |
| **偏離的後果** | 自己在防禦上開出缺口，變成被攻擊的目標 |

他認為根本目標永遠是**直接攻向對手的中線**，也就是最關鍵的部位（頭、頸）。只要偏離這個原則，就等於自己在防禦上開出缺口，把自己變成對手的攻擊目標，而不是進攻目標。

### 機會動作變主攻 = 災難

To break with this commandment entails following the movements of the opponents actions rather than perusing the primary centerline targets that are the head and neck. This is an easy sin to make when trying to learn chance action fighting; it takes a correct combination of actions and events to execute a chance technique properly and safely.

違背這條禁令的表現往往是**跟隨對手的動作**而不是**追擊對手的中線**——把注意力放在對手的移動上，而不是直接打擊頭頸。這是學習機會動作時最容易犯的錯，因為正確執行機會技巧需要動作與時機的**正確配合**。

Making a chance action into a primary technique will spell disaster against a seasoned opponent. It will provide a waiting bridge for the enemy to cross, or will open a gap in one's defenses that may be impossible to close once violated.

把機會動作當成主要攻擊的結果往往是災難：

| 後果 | Consequence |
|------|-------------|
| 給對手留下可跨越的橋手 | Provides a waiting bridge for the enemy |
| 在防禦上留下無法彌補的缺口 | Opens a gap impossible to close |

> Sigung Wong Shun Leung would say that training anything else other than how to attack and stop the enemy is training to be a target.

> 黃師公會說，只練習如何攻擊與阻止敵人之外的任何事，都是在練**成為被攻擊的目標**。

---

## 結語 — 格鬥就是賭博

Thus in our tradition chance actions serve to provide a deeper experience and a more developed background to a persons training, broadening their options and honing the primary attacking techniques.

因此，在我們的訓練裡，機會行為的作用是**深化練習體驗、擴大技術底蘊**，讓練習者的選擇更多、主攻技巧更鋒利。

> **Fighting is gambling**; Wing Chun teaches that training must be about putting the odds in your favor as much as possible.

> **格鬥就是賭博**；詠春的訓練就是要盡量把勝算往自己這邊傾斜。

Wing Chun accomplishes this goal in its training experience by being as direct in action, simple in solution and economical in movement as possible.

為了達成這個目標，詠春要求：

| 原則 | Principle |
|------|-----------|
| **動作直接** | Direct in action |
| **解決方式簡單** | Simple in solution |
| **動作經濟** | Economical in movement |

用最少的力、最直接的路徑完成最有效的攻擊。
