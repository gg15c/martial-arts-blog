詠春力量訓練 — WCK Power Training

February 20, 2025 · 10 min

By Gregory E. LeBlanc

先有勇氣、再有力量，最後講技術#

In Wing Chun Kung Fu there is a saying “First courage, second power and then technique”, this reflects a greater importance put on the willingness to fight and on an ability to make techniques profitable.

在詠春拳裡有句口訣說 「先有勇氣、再有力量，最後講技術」。這句話的重點是：敢打敢拼比單純練技巧更重要，只有先有「勇」才能把招式發揮出效益。

詠春力量的來源#

Power development in Wing Chun Kung Fu is not primarily based on muscular strength but rather on knowledge of body mechanics, joint/tendon strength and using the opponent’s own committed power against them. This joint/tendon-based power is termed structural power and is the key to understanding how to transmit power using Wing Chun footwork and in the old days was one of the inner circle secrets of Wing Chun.

詠春的力量並不是靠大塊肌肉練出來的，而是靠身體機械的原理、關節與肌腱的韌性，進一步利用對手自己的力量來對抗他自己。這種靠關節、肌腱產生的力量叫結構力量，也是詠春腳步把力量傳遞出去的關鍵，過去甚至是詠春內部的祕密之一。

詠春力量的三大來源#

In Wing Chun power comes from a joint supported alignment with the ground, or is derived from the motion and speed of the body (momentum). Power in Wing Chun also depends on strong emotion (called Geng Ging) creating an explosive, vicious attack.

在詠春裡，力量的來源有：

來源Source說明
地面支撐Joint supported alignment with the ground腳踩實地、身體與地面形成的支撐角度
慣性衝力Motion and speed of the body (momentum)身體的移動速度，把慣性轉成衝擊
驚勁Strong emotion (Geng Ging)強烈情緒讓攻擊變得爆發、狠勁

力量的兩種形式#

The type of power used against the target also can be different, usually taking the form either of a penetrating or percussive force.

類型Type說明
穿透式Penetrating force力量穿過目標
衝擊式Percussive force力量在表面爆發

Regardless of the type of power and its implementation, power must be trained and applied in a controlled, automatic and natural way.

不管是哪種力量，都必須在受控、自動、自然的狀態下練習與使用，才能避免失控傷人。

六點半棍 — 力量訓練的核心#

To this end Wing Chun has developed a variety of methods to promote and train striking power, chief among them is working with the Luk Dim Boon Gwan (The Six and a one half point pole or Dragon Pole). The advantage to using the Dragon Pole is that it naturally coordinates various qualities needed to maximize power; thus training the student to use the body, breath and mind all in one action.

為了培養這樣的力量，詠春發展了許多訓練方法，其中最核心的就是 「六點半棍」或稱「龍棍」。使用六點半棍的好處在於，它本身就能把身體、呼吸、意念三者同時協調起來，讓學員在一次動作裡同時鍛鍊出發力所需的所有條件，從而把力量發揮到最大。

結構力量與力量點#

Deriving striking power from an alignment with the ground or from the body alignment and mass in motion is generally referred to as structural power. Structural power is an understanding of the concept of Power Points on the body and how they relate to using the angle of the joints in unison.

從腳踏在地面、或是身體對齊、質量在移動時產生的擊力，通常會稱作 「結構力量」。「結構力量」其實就是在說 「力量點」 這個概念——每個力量點都對應身體的 12 大關節，只要把這些關節的角度同時調整好、穩固住，就能把力量最大限度地從身體傳到目標。

12 大關節的 6 個關鍵力量點#

Power Points are key areas on the body associated with the 12 major joints that when stabilized correctly provide maximum transmission of force through the body and into the target. The critical power points are the ankles, knees, hips, shoulders, elbows and especially the wrists.

力量點Power Point重要性
腳踝Ankles地面與身體的連接點
膝蓋Knees下盤穩定的關鍵
髖關節Hips上下半身力量的樞紐
肩膀Shoulders手臂力量的起點
手肘Elbows力量傳遞的中繼站
手腕Wrists⭐ 最關鍵 — 力量的最終出口

The trick to using Power Points correctly is a major part of the fundamental concepts and skills that are the corner stone of Wing Chun’s power theory and practice.

正確運用這些點，是詠春拳力量理論與實踐的根基。

Practiced in a natural and simultaneous way these concepts of structure and power points allow the practitioner an ability to access force for striking that is not totally dependent on muscular power.

若能以自然、同步的方式練習這些結構與力量點的概念，練功者就能在不完全依賴肌肉力量的前提下，仍然產生足夠、有效的打擊力量。

In the interest of developing structural power, correct breathing and the proper state of mind the Wing Chun practitioner trains with the 9’ long Dragon Pole.

因此，為了培養結構力量、學會正確呼吸、調整心態，詠春拳的練習者會用 9 呎長的六點半棍來進行專門的訓練。

六點半棍的歷史與構造#

現代六點半棍#

The modern Dragon Pole is typically constructed out of a single piece of hard wood such as Purple Heart, giving it great weight, strength and flexibility.

現在的六點半棍大多是用一整塊硬木（像紫心木）做成的，重量又沉、很結實、彈性也好。

起源 — 船夫的長棍#

Its history links it to the long poles used by boatmen in rural China, they would use their poles to guide and manipulate cargo boats in the shallow rivers of old China.

這根棍子的歷史可以追溯到古時中國鄉下的船夫，他們會用長棍在淺河裡指引、操控貨船。

少林的交流#

Wing Chun Dragon Pole became an adaptation of ancient pole fighting training that was passed onto its masters through mutually beneficial relationships with teachers of older Shaolin derivative styles. The pole actions were grafted onto the existing Wing Chun system and some say practiced in secret by Wing Chun masters in hiding traveling on riverboats.

詠春拳的六點半棍其實是從古老的棍術改編過來的。當初詠春的大師跟較早的少林系統老師互相交流、互惠互利，才把棍法傳給詠春。這些棍子的動作被「嫁接」到詠春的既有體系裡，甚至有人說，當年詠春高手在躲避清軍時，會偷偷坐在河船上練習。

水中練棍#

Even today one of the training techniques for the Dragon Pole involves moving heavy wood floating on the surface of a pool.

即使在今天，其中一種練習棍法的方式是，把一塊沉重的木頭丟在水池裡，並嘗試使用棍移動它。

六點半棍的特質#

Dragon Pole applications tend to mimic the open hand usage of Wing Chun, they are direct, powerful no-nonsense actions designed with the quick and violent realities of combat in mind (typically used with a pole of fighting length around 6-7’).

六點半棍的招式大都模仿詠春拳的手法：

特質Quality說明
直接Direct沒有花俏、多餘的動作
有力Powerful全身結構力量貫穿棍身
務實No-nonsense為真實搏鬥設計
快狠準Quick and violent配合近身格鬥的需求
實戰棍長Fighting length約 6-7 尺

現代用途 — 強化結構力量#

Modern Wing Chun practice develops the Dragon Pole less for its application as a weapon and more for its perfect compliment to the reinforcement and the promotion of structural power. The end benefit is that Dragon Pole training offers a simultaneous positive resistance to the bodies Power Points and at the same time develops control and application of force for Wing Chun open hand techniques.

在現代的詠春拳練習裡，六點半棍已不再主要當作武器使用，而是加強結構力量的好幫手。練六點半棍的好處是，它同時給身體的力量點提供正向的阻力，還能培養手法的控制力與力量運用，讓拳路更穩、更有威力。

內力 — Internal Power#

Sifu Gary Lam of Monterey Park California describes the Dragon Pole as critical training for the development of what he calls Internal Power.

加州蒙特利公園的林師父告訴我們，六點半棍是培養他所說的 「內力」 的關鍵訓練工具。

什麼是內力？#

Internal Power is a term used to describe the use of the structural framework of the body in motion, accompanied by a deliberate mental and emotional intent to one point of expression; in this case that one point becomes the last 12cm or so of the working end of the Dragon Pole.

內力指的是身體在運動時的結構框架，配合有意識、帶情緒的意圖，把力量集中在一個點上——這個點就在六點半棍最末端、約 12 公分左右的地方。

「嚇人的詠春」#

He often demonstrates Internal Power by exhibiting a devastating marshaling of power in the many strikes and projection techniques of Wing Chun, a way of doing Wing Chun that a senior Wing Chun practitioner and student of Sifu Lam’s dubbed “scary Wing Chun”.

林師父常常用六點半棍示範內力，他在詠春手法的各種擊打與推等動作裡，能一次性釋放出驚人的威力。這種打法被他的資深學生稱為 「嚇人的詠春」，因為看起來非常凶猛。

四大指標#

Internal Power becomes the sum total of the bodies force, guided and authenticated by the traditional marks of success, that is: Jun (stability), Fai (speed), Wan (accuracy) and Geng (power). These four qualities of success are what Sifu Lam defines as the merits of mastery in any art, expressed in a seamless combination with all of Wing Chun’s actions.

在林師父看來，內力就是身體力量的總和，而這個力量要靠傳統的四大指標來驗證：

特質Quality說明
準（Jun）Precision擊中的精準度
穩（Wan）Stability身體的穩定性
快（Fai）Speed出招的速度
勁（Geng）Power出招的勁道

這四項素質是林師父認為任何功夫「精通」的標準，也正是詠春所有動作要完整融合的要素。

六點半棍訓練流程#

第一階段 — 基礎#

Dragon Pole training begins by learning the correct way to hold and heft the pole, if done incorrectly it becomes easy for the pole to be knocked from your hands and difficult to correctly express the bodies structural power through the pole.

六點半棍的訓練一開始先教學員正確的握棍與舉棍方式。如果握法或舉法不對，棍子很容易被打掉，身體的結構力量也就無法順利從棍子傳遞出來。

Then the student is given various lines of techniques to perform, these cover the basic techniques of Dragon Pole and introduce the footwork methods used throughout the training.

接下來，林師父會傳授多種棍法學員練習，這些技術涵蓋六點半棍的基礎招式，同時會加入在整個訓練過程中會用到的腳步走法。

第二階段 — 箭拳與單腿練習#

At this stage the Arrow Punching exercise and single leg form training are introduced, these practices develop whole body power in motion and train leg strength and balance.

在這個階段會加入 「箭拳」 練習以及 「單腿」 練習：

練習Exercise訓練目標
箭拳Arrow Punching全身在動作中產生力量
單腿練習Single leg form腿部力量與平衡感

第三階段 — 套路#

After a sufficient amount of time has gone by, allowing the student to digest the introductory material, the form training is introduced.

等學員把基礎內容練熟、消化完之後，才會進入套路的練習。

The Dragon Pole form is where it gets its name as the “Six and one half point pole”; this is because there are six main strikes in the form and a half strike. There are other actions in the form besides these, but these six and one half strikes are considered the main lesson of the Dragon Pole form.

六點半棍名稱的由來是因為套路中有六個主要擊棍法，外加一個半擊棍的動作。雖然套路裡還有其他動作，但這六個全擊加半擊被視為最核心的課題。

陰裡有陽 — 放鬆中帶剛#

The form should be done with a relaxed firmness (Yin with Yang inside), harnessing the structural power of the body behind all pole actions.

練這套路時，要求 「放鬆中帶剛」（亦即「陰裡有陽」），把身體的結構力量全部應用在每一個棍法上。

林師父的核心觀點#

Sifu Lam considerers the Dragon Pole essential for learning empty hand structural power and also teaches that it is through the pole training that the student truly learns to be both yielding and explosive as you react to an opponents attack.

林師父認為六點半棍是學習詠春 徒手結構力量的關鍵。他主張，正是透過棍法的訓練，學員才能真正體會在面對對手攻擊時，既能順勢柔軟、又能瞬間爆發。

陰陽互補的訓練哲學#

Sifu Lam teaches that in training any single quality such as power; its opposite quality should first be promoted. Thus Dragon Pole training while initially training one to be powerful ultimately teaches us to be receptive, soft and yielding (called Yang with Yin inside). This is the key to the correct control and expression of power, if we fix our action or power then it will not have a way to naturally maximize its potential.

林師父還強調，練功如果只著重於某一項特質（例如「勁」），應先把它的對立面（柔軟、接受）先培養起來，兩者互補，才能形成完整的力量。

訓練階段表面目標實際目標
初期變得有勁學會發力
最終接受、柔軟、順從陽裡有陰 — 正確控制力量

若只固定在某種力量或某個動作上，力量就無法自然地發揮出最大的效能。

雙人練習與棍樁#

雙人練習#

Training continues with practicing two person drills and utilizing the standing or hanging Dragon Pole dummy. Two person training gives the practitioner an opportunity to get first hand experience with how the techniques will work in actual combat and also introduces the ideas of deflection, misdirection and evasion.

訓練會接著進入雙人練習，同時使用站立式或吊掛式的六點半棍樁。雙人練習讓學員能直接體會招式在實戰中會怎麼發揮，並且把化解、誘導、閃避的概念帶進去。

訓練內容Training目的
雙人對練Two person drills實戰應用、化解與閃避
棍樁練習Dragon Pole dummy精準使用力量、打固定或移動目標
自由對打Controlled free sparring隨機、自然地施展技術

As with empty hand training, the goal is being able to execute techniques in a random, un-rehearsed and natural way, this is trained through controlled free sparring with the poles.

就像手法練習一樣，目標是要在隨機、未排練、自然的情況下，能把技術完整、正確地施展出來。這種感覺是靠可控的自由對打，用兩根棍子互相對練，讓動作既有節制又不失真實感。

棍樁練習#

Dragon Pole dummy training focuses on developing the accurate use of power and further develops the student’s use of structural force against a solid and moving target.

六點半棍樁的練習重點在於精準使用力量，同時讓學員學會怎麼把結構性的力量對準固定或會動的目標。

高階訓練#

Advanced levels of all form and drill training end by linking the various techniques into spontaneous, free flowing combinations of techniques.

當所有形、套路與技術練到高階時，最終會把各種動作自然串連，形成即興、流暢的組合招式，讓整套系統變得更有彈性、更實用。

盤手與八斬刀#

盤手（Pun Sao）#

Wing Chun power training is continued further by practicing the Pun Sao drill and eventually practicing the Baat Jaam Do (Double 8 cut knife).

詠春的力量訓練接下來會練 「盤手」 這套功夫，最後再練 「八斬刀」。

The Pun Sao drill is a variation of the basic Qi Sao training (Wing Chun free sparring training), and as in Qi Sao is begun with the arms interlocked and the legs positioned in a training stance. In Pun Sao the goal is to learn how to harness and apply structural power in Qi Sao, this is done by an emphases on the sitting (structural alignment with the ground) and moving (body mass in motion) power as pressure is applied to the opponents centerline.

盤手其實是基礎「黐手」訓練的一個變化版本。剛開始練黐手時，雙手會交叉緊扣、雙腳站在基本的練功姿勢。而盤手的目的在於學會把結構力量運用到黐手裡：

力量類型Type說明
坐（Sitting）結構對齊身體與地面的正確對齊
動（Moving）慣性力量身體的質量在移動時產生的力量

Adding a double hand pull upon retreating expands on this practice, followed by your opponent taking your position in response to the pull. Like all other drills this eventually becomes a animated and free flowing practice, training to naturally apply structural power and respond to changes in position and balance naturally.

在練習的過程中，會加入雙手向後拉的動作，然後讓對手在你拉回的同時「接上」你的位置，這樣的來回拉扯不斷延伸練習的深度。和其他所有的套路一樣，盤手最終會演變成一種活潑、流暢的對打，讓練功者自然地把結構力量帶入實戰，並且隨著站位與重心的變化自動調整。

八斬刀（Baat Jaam Do）#

The Baat Jaam Do training develops tight, focused and powerful handwork, further engendering the correct flavor and application of structurally correct Wing Chun.

而八斬刀的訓練則專注於培養緊密、集中且有力的手法，同時把詠春的結構概念完整地體現在手刀上，使得刀的招式既符合詠春的結構原理，又能發揮出刀法的獨特威力。

結語 — 從簡單到複雜，再回到簡單#

Wing Chun was designed to provide a direct and powerful answer to the fast paced and unpredictable realities of hand-to-hand combat. Its system was built on fundamental concepts that can be found at all levels of training, making the outward appearance of its techniques secondary to its inner structural standards.

詠春拳的設計目的本來就是要在快節奏、變化莫測的徒手格鬥裡，給予一個直接、又有威力的方法。這套系統是依靠基本概念建構的，這些概念在各個練習階段都能看到，因而外在招式的樣子只是次要，真正重要的是內在的結構。

Sifu Lam describes the journey of Wing Chun training as going from the simple to the complex and in the end back to the simple again; advanced Wing Chun develops not from its outward form but rather from a deepening of the essential concepts, structure and skills.

林師父把詠春的修行過程比喻成 「從簡單到複雜，再回到簡單」 ；高手的詠春不在於外形變得多花俏，而是把基本概念、身體結構與技巧不斷深化，最後回到最純粹、最有效的打法。

傳承與革新#

Wing Chun not only reflects the qualities of a high standard traditional Chinese martial art, but also adapts as part of the changing modern world of fighting arts. Sigung Wong Shun Leung and Sifu Lam have both made alterations to the form of Wing Chun that was based on their evolving experience of the martial arts, but always preserving its core concepts, skills and fundamental structure.

詠春不只是高水準的傳統中國武術，它也會隨著現代格鬥環境的變化而調整。師公黃淳樑與林師父都根據自己不斷累積的實戰經驗，對詠春的形體作出些微調整，但始終保留核心概念、基本功與結構，不會改壞根本。

詠春的血與肉#

It is the tradition of structure, concepts and skills of Wing Chun that defines its heart and soul, and holds the integrity of its theory and power in place. To this end it is the hope and goal of Masters like Sifu Lam that the heart and soul of Wing Chun be passed down to the next generation, that heart and soul is Wing Chun’s true power and legacy.

在詠春裡，結構、概念與技能共同構成了它的血與肉，也是它理論完整性與力量真諦的根本。正因如此，像林師父這樣的前輩期盼且努力，要把詠春的血與肉傳給下一代，因為那才是真正的東西。
