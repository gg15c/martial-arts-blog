詠春黐手訓練 — Wing Chun Qi Sao Training

March 1, 2025 · 11 min

By Gregory E. LeBlanc

詠春的誕生#

Several hundred years ago a martial art was born in southern China, its founders pooled their collective knowledge into a uniform method of training allowing its practitioners to benefit from generations of martial arts development. The training was designed to transmit only the best of their combined experience, leaving out anything that was not directly applicable to combat.

幾百年前，一種武術在中國南方誕生。它的創立者們將畢生所學匯聚成一套統一的訓練方法，讓後世練習者能從世代武術發展的結晶中獲益。這套訓練旨在傳授他們最精華的實戰經驗，剔除任何不能直接應用於戰鬥的部分。

This system deliberately began with the most fundamental, streamlined and central concepts of martial knowledge, eventually bringing its students to the most advanced methods and abilities. The core purpose in training was always the same, to stop the enemy with the most direct, economical and simple means possible.

這個系統刻意從最基礎、精簡且核心的武術概念開始，最終引領學生掌握最進階的方法與能力。訓練的核心目的始終如一：用最直接、經濟且簡單的方式制止敵人。

歷史淵源#

Tradition holds that this martial art was developed to fight off the invading Qing dynasty. The training being specifically designed to counter the techniques of Qing soldiers, and applied primarily for urban guerrilla warfare and political assassination.

傳統認為，這門武術是為了對抗入侵的清朝而發展出來的。其訓練專為反制清兵的技術而設計，主要應用於城市游擊戰與政治暗殺。

Also another oral tradition links it’s founding to martial arts masters from the southern Shaolin Chan Buddhist temple, and then later being disseminated by their disciples through the traveling Red Junk Opera.

另一個口耳相傳的說法是，它的創立與南方少林禪宗寺廟的武術大師有關，隨後由他們的弟子透過巡迴的紅船戲班傳播開來。

Whatever the styles original and true history is, Wing Chun Kung Fu has become one of the worlds best known and most widely practiced martial arts of the 21st century. Perhaps its chief distinguishing feature is the training practice known as Qi Sao (sticky hands), often called the soul of Wing Chun.

無論這門武術真實的歷史起源為何，詠春拳已成為 21 世紀全球最知名且練習人數最多的武術之一。或许它最主要的特色就是被稱為詠春靈魂的訓練方式——黐手 （Qi Sao）。

黐手 — 從概念到實戰的橋樑#

Qi sao is the bridge in Wing Chun training from learning concepts and techniques, to putting them into practice and then ultimately being able to fight with them. It provides a platform from which the practitioner can safely develop his/her abilities and test the abilities of others.

黐手是詠春訓練中，從學習概念與技術，過渡到實際應用，並最終能在實戰中發揮的橋樑。它提供了一個平台，讓練習者能安全地發展自身能力並測試對手的能力。

初期訓練架構#

In its early stages Qi Sao training is done in a prescribed manner, usually practiced without the benefit of footwork techniques. Each practitioner begins by positioning his/her arms into a forward locked position (called fok, tan and bon); the legs are placed in a parallel, triangular training position.

在初期階段，黐手訓練是以固定的方式進行，通常不涉及步法。每位練習者首先將雙臂置於前向鎖定的姿勢：

手法Technique
伏手Fok
攤手Tan
膀手Bon

雙腿則處於平行的三角形訓練站姿（二字拑羊馬）。

基礎技法#

From this training stance each practitioner performs a variety of techniques; such as Cover hand, Pak Sao, Lap Sao and Tan Da to name only a few.

從這個訓練架勢開始，練習者會演練各種技術：

技法Technique
護手Cover hand
拍手Pak Sao
擸手Lap Sao
攤打Tan Da

進階發展#

Also the more dynamic free style Qi Sao practice is developed from this position, providing the basis for all of the random two person training including the brutal Qi Gerk training (sticky leg) and eventually free sparring (San Sao).

更具動態的自由黐手也是從這個姿勢發展而來，為所有隨機的雙人訓練奠定基礎：

訓練Training說明
自由黐手Free style Qi Sao隨機雙人訓練的基礎
黐腳Qi Gerk (sticky leg)激烈的腿法訓練
散手San Sao (free sparring)最終的自由對打

陰陽手 — 最高階的黐手技能#

At its most advanced levels Qi Sao becomes an exercise in the training of skills such as control and feeling, the yin and yang hands (soft/tight) and developing the ability to fight naturally.

在最高階的層次中，黐手變成一種訓練 控制與感覺、陰陽手 （柔／剛） 以及培養自然戰鬥能力的練習。

Of paramount importance in a Wing Chun student’s advancement in Qi Sao training is the development of the Yin and Yang hands (yin within yang and yang within yin), the two hands being used in opposite ways for the same action.

在詠春學生的黐手進階訓練裡，最重要的一環是發展陰陽手 （陰中有陽，陽中有陰），也就是雙手在同一動作中以相反的方式運作。

陰陽手的應用#

An example of this would be making one hand tight and the other soft, such as when using the hands in combination for a simultaneous attack and defense action. Other examples of yin/yang hands are:

應用Application
一手剛、一手柔同時進行攻防動作
干擾與攻擊的組合Disturb and attack combination
轉換發力點的重心Switching the power point emphasis
改變對手朝向並同時攻擊Changing the opponent’s correct facing combined with an attack
柔剛互換Becoming soft/tight when facing tight/soft
靈活切換雙手狀態Being able to switch between both hands as needed

黐手的最高境界 — 你就是詠春#

The highest levels of Qi Sao practice rarely involves striking, and always focuses on the natural fighting style developed when a practitioner has mastered the form of his/her art and now acts and re-acts in a flowing, spontaneous manner.

最高境界的黐手練習極少涉及擊打，而是始終專注於練習者在精通其武術形式後，所展現出流暢、自發的自然戰鬥風格。

Grandmaster Yip Man was said to never have used crossing hand techniques (i.e. striking) in Qi Sao, rather he would only control the action and movement of his opponent.

據說葉問宗師在黐手時從不使用交手技術（即擊打），他只會控制對手的動作與移動。

黐手不是打鬥#

Qi Sao practice is considered a kind of playing; it is definitely not a form of street fighting. Nor is Qi Sao a type of challenge fighting (beimo), Qi Sao is a friendly training match between fellow practitioners.

黐手練習被視為一種「遊玩」（玩手），絕對不是街頭打鬥。黐手也不是一種挑戰賽（比武，Beimo），它是同門之間友好的訓練切磋。

It is said that being a good fighter does not also mean being good at Qi Sao, but if you can become proficient in both then you have a special ability indeed.

常言道，一個好的格鬥者未必擅長黐手，但如果你能精通兩者，那你確實具備了非凡的能力。

四種接觸性訓練#

Ultimately contact training in Wing Chun can be broken down into 4 separate categories, of which Qi Sao is only one part:

最終，詠春的接觸性訓練可以分為四個不同的類別，而黐手只是其中之一：

層次Level類別Category
第一層次Level 1雙人技術訓練Two person technique training
第二層次Level 2黐手（黏手）Qi Sao (sticky hands)
第三層次Level 3比武（挑戰賽）Beimo (challenge fighting)
第四層次Level 4生死搏鬥Fighting for one’s life

These 4 levels categorize the main differences in how a Wing Chun practitioner views his/her contact training experience. This ranges from pre-arranged practice to a life or death situation, of course fighting for your life is not technically a training experience. But it is important to mention because the mind set is very different at each of these levels.

這四個層次劃分了詠春練習者如何看待其接觸性訓練經驗的主要差異。這涵蓋了從預先安排好的練習，到生死存亡的危急關頭。當然，為生存而戰在嚴格意義上並不算是訓練經驗。但這點值得一提，因為在每個層次中，心態是截然不同的。

第一層次 — 雙人技術訓練#

In the first level of contact training the basic movements and concepts of Wing Chun are practiced with a partner. There is no feeling of competition or of anything to win or lose. This stage involves learning and developing the various sets of techniques and drills, such as the well-known Pak Sao counter drills (partially shown by Bruce Lee in Enter the Dragon).

在接觸性訓練的第一層次，主要是與夥伴練習詠春的基本動作與概念。這裡沒有競爭的感覺，也沒有輸贏之分。這個階段包括學習與發展各種技術套路和反覆練習，例如著名的拍手反擊訓練（李小龍在電影《龍爭虎鬥》中曾部分展示）。

Here we develop the basic habits of Wing Chun:

在這裡，我們養成詠春的基本習慣：

基本習慣Basic Habit
從地面發力 （扎馬）Sitting from the ground with our movements
沉肘Keeping the elbows down
控制中線Controlling the centerline
正面朝向對手Facing our opponent squarely

This is the foundation for building the skills that define Wing Chun, if this level of learning is skimmed over, you may appear to be doing Wing Chun, but the later actions of Qi Sao will lack the qualities and habits that are at the heart of properly practiced Wing Chun.

這是建立詠春核心技能的基礎；如果草率帶過這個學習階段，你可能表面上看起來在打詠春，但日後在黐手時，將會缺乏正確練習詠春所必須具備的核心特質與習慣。

第二層次 — 黐手（Qi Sao）#

The second level of Wing Chun contact training brings in the elements of wanting to make the hit (a.k.a. crossing hand) and to not be hit; here the classic Qi Sao training takes place. Ego rears it’s ugly head and the full force of Wing Chun character development begins to work it’s magic.

詠春接觸性訓練的第二層次，帶入了「想要擊中對手（即交手）」與「不被擊中」的元素；經典的黐手訓練就在此階段展開。自負心開始作祟，而詠春品格發展的全部力量也開始施展它的魔法。

每個人都必須走過同一條路#

My teacher Sifu Gary Lam (Lam Man Hoc) always says that everyone must go by the same road, and Qi Sao for most of us is when the road starts getting bumpy.

我的師傅林文學總是說，每個人都必須走過同一條路，而對我們大多數人來說，黐手就是這條路開始變得崎嶇的地方。

最常見的錯誤#

The most common mistake made here is viewing this stage of training as fighting. Qi Sao should be a little playful, trained with a generous spirit. Sifu Lam says that you need to have a big heart to do Qi Sao effectively.

這個階段最常見的錯誤，就是把這項訓練當成真正的打鬥。黐手應該帶點趣味，並抱持著寬宏的心胸來訓練。林師傅說，你需要有一顆寬廣的心才能有效率地練習黐手。

控制 vs 擊打#

At this level the problem is not being able to hit someone, but rather can you control and feel his or her actions instead. Sifu Lam says this control of our opponent is the key to progressing to higher levels of ability in Wing Chun; the techniques used advances from striking our opponent only, to more sophisticated ways of fighting.

在這個層次，問題不在於你能不能打中別人，而在於你是否能控制並感知對方的動作。林師傅表示，控制對手是邁向詠春更高層次能力的關鍵；所使用的技術會從單純擊打對手，進化為更複雜精緻的戰鬥方式。

控制距離與力量#

Additionally doing Qi sao in this fashion involves controlling the distance and power applied in practice, if we trained with uncontrolled strikes, Qi Sao would not last very long and dental bills would be very high. Thus we not only strive to control our opponent’s actions, but equally important we endeavor to develop an ability to control our own actions as well.

此外，以這種方式進行黐手，包含了在練習中控制距離與發力；如果我們用不受控制的擊打來訓練，黐手將無法長久進行，且牙醫費用會非常驚人。因此，我們不僅要努力控制對手的動作，同樣重要的是，我們必須致力培養控制自身動作的能力。

第六感般的感知力#

The skilled practitioner on first contact with his/her training partners arms can sense their strengths and weaknesses, being able to feel their physical structure and level of development immediately. A master of Qi Sao can predict an opponents every move, using a highly developed ability that almost seems to mimic a sixth sense.

熟練的練習者在與訓練夥伴的手臂一接觸時，就能感知到對方的優缺點，能立即感覺出對方的身體結構與訓練水平。黐手大師能預測對手的每一個動作，這種高度發展的能力幾乎就像是第六感。

智力與狡詰的較量#

Qi Sao is as much a contest of cunning and a test of intelligence, as it is a physical challenge of skill.

黐手不僅是一場體能與技巧的挑戰，同樣也是智力與狡詰的較量。

Sifu Lam continually emphasizes throughout the Qi Sao training experience that without being able to control our own mind, emotions and actions, as well as control our opponent’s actions, the higher levels of Wing Chun usage will be beyond our grasp.

林師傅在整個黐手訓練過程中不斷強調：如果無法控制我們自己的心智、情緒與動作，也無法控制對手的動作，我們將無法觸及詠春更高的應用境界。

第三層次 — 比武（Beimo）#

The third level of contact training in Wing Chun is were things acquire a significantly different nature, we leave the protected world of true training and venture into less than charted lands. This level represents an actual fight, but this fight is arranged ahead of time and is between trained participants (Beimo or skill comparison). Here there is a real danger of injury, people can loose teeth and get bones broken.

詠春接觸性訓練的第三層次，是事物本質發生顯著改變的地方。我們離開了受保護的純訓練世界，冒險進入未知的領域。這個層次代表了真正的打鬥，但這場打鬥是事先安排好的，且雙方都是受過訓練的參與者（比武，或稱技術切磋）。在這裡有受傷的真實危險，人們可能會被打斷骨頭或掉牙齒。

講手王 — 黃淳樑#

This is the activity that after 60 victories in beimo made the late Wong Shun Leung (Sigung) so famous, having been given the nickname “king of the challenge fight”.

正是這種活動，讓已故的黃淳樑師公在贏得了 60 場比武後聲名大噪，獲得了講手王 （比武之王）的稱號。

It was also the real brutality of beimo that convinced Sigung Wong to eventually stop fighting, having accidentally blinded his final opponents left eye.

也是因為比武的真實殘酷性，使黃師公最終決定停止挑戰，因為他曾意外導致最後一位對手的左眼失明。

比武的本質#

Under generally controlled conditions and among opponents who respect one another, beimo can be the ultimate experience in testing a practitioner’s skill. This is not street fighting, but the control that was exercised in Qi Sao training is put aside. We want to win against, but not seriously injure our honored opponent.

黐手（Qi Sao）比武（Beimo）
性質友好訓練真實戰鬥
力量控制✅ 控制力量❌ 放開控制
目標控制與學習贏得勝利
傷害意圖❌ 不傷害贏但不嚴重傷害
危險程度低高（可能斷骨掉牙）

在一般受控的條件下，且雙方相互尊重的情況下，比武可以是測試練習者技能的終極體驗。這不是街頭鬥毆，但黐手訓練中對力量的控制在這裡將被擱置。我們渴望獲勝，但不想嚴重傷害我們尊敬的對手。

Some of Sigung Wong’s life long friends were former beimo opponents, having gained mutual respect and admiration for one another through the experience.

黃師公的一些畢生摯友就曾經是比武的對手，他們透過這種經歷贏得了彼此的相互尊重與欽佩。

第四層次 — 生死搏鬥#

The last level of contact training is not really training at all, unless of course you happen to be a professional soldier. This level is mentioned only because in each of the three above categories the attitude was different; here the experience gained from hard training is summoned in a cruel totality.

接觸性訓練的最後一個層次其實根本不是訓練，除非你剛好是名職業軍人。之所以提及這個層次，是因為在前面三個類別中，心態都是不同的；而在這裡，從艱苦訓練中獲得的經驗將以殘酷的整體形式被召喚出來。

四個層次的心態對比#

層次心態目標
第一層次開放、接受掌握基本技術
第二層次寬宏精神訓練與技能發展，與自己競爭
第三層次自由搏擊獲取經驗，非故意傷害
第四層次冷酷無情、報復性保衛生命，消滅敵人

In the first level we had an open, receptive approach; our goal was to master the basics of technique. In the second level we ideally wanted to have a generous spirit focused on training and development of skill; the only person you should have been competing with was yourself. The third level can be called a kind of free fighting tournament experience (beimo); rules or rounds do not govern this stage of training. Beimo is an actual fight, but the big difference is that the participants fight for the experience of it, not to deliberately injure or maim the opponent. Accidents can happen in this fighting environment of full power strikes’; this is as dangerous as so-called “training” can get.

在第一層次中，我們採取開放、接受的態度；目標是掌握基本技術。在第二層次中，我們理想上希望保持寬宏的精神，專注於訓練與技能發展；你唯一應該競爭的對象是自己。第三層次可被稱為一種自由搏擊的比賽經驗（比武）；規則或回合並不適用於此階段的訓練。比武是真實的戰鬥，但最大的差別在於參與者是為了獲得經驗而戰，而不是為了故意傷害或使對手殘缺。在這種全力擊打的戰鬥環境中，意外是可能發生的；這是所謂「訓練」所能達到最危險的地步。

鏢指 — 緊急技術#

The last category of the four types of contact training is mentioned here for the purpose of discussion only. The mind set here is appropriate only when defending your life, for whatever reason. The practitioner adopts a cruel, vengeful attitude. This is using Wing Chun in its most direct and powerful way, here you will see emergency techniques from the formerly secret Bil Jee form, designed for one thing only, to neutralize our enemy!

這四種類型中的最後一個類別在此提出僅為了討論目的。這裡的心態只有在為了保衛生命（無論出於何種原因）時才適用。練習者會採取冷酷無情、報復性的態度。這是以最直接、最具破壞力的方式使用詠春；在這裡，你會看到過去作為不傳之秘的鏢指套路中的緊急技術，這些技術只為了一個目的：消滅我們的敵人！

This is life or death, kill or be killed. Here words like butcher and destroy come to mind, this is the tooth and nail of human nature, honed to a fine edge through years of training.

這是生與死，不是殺就是被殺。在這裡，腦海中浮現的是屠宰與毀滅等字眼，這是人類本性的激烈拼殺，經過多年的訓練被磨礪至極致。

This kind of application is probably most like the original intentions for Wing Chun usage, that being for revolution, revenge and assassination. As members of a good and civil society, and dedicating to fostering the same, these kinds of techniques are not emphasized in normal training and are taught only to advanced students.

這類的應用大概最接近詠春的原始意圖——即用於革命、復仇與暗殺。身為優良公民社會的一員，並致力於促進社會和諧，這類技術在一般訓練中不會被強調，僅傳授給高階學生。

來留去送，甩手直衝#

Qi Sao as it is practiced in Sifu Lam’s school provides an opportunity to develop, enhance and as he calls it “upgrade” our abilities.

在林師傅的武館中練習的黐手，提供了一個發展、增強並如他所說升級我們能力的機會。

詠春拳訣的精髓#

One of the most profound examples of this, and possibly the highest ideal trained for in an entire career of practice, is the essence of the Wing Chun song “loi lou hoi sung, lat sao chay chun”.

其中最深遠的例子之一，也可能是一生練習所追求的最高理想，就是詠春拳訣的精髓：

來留去送，甩手直衝

Roughly translated this refers to the ultimate accomplishment in the Wing Chun fighting style; to embrace/hold what comes, escort/send off what leaves and when contact is lost attack to the center/weak point immediately.

拳訣意義
來留擁抱並留住來勢
去送順勢送走去勢
甩手直衝當失去接觸時，立即向中心與弱點發起攻擊

只能在壓力下鍛鍊#

This lofty skill can only be developed under pressure, in the crucible of Qi Sao.

這項崇高的技能只能在壓力下、在黐手的熔爐中培養出來。

Being able to apply it at will, going with and guiding your opponent, fighting in the natural style of an expert who has transcended the form of his art, this is the highest accomplishment realized in action.

能夠隨心所欲地應用它，順應並引導你的對手，以一位超越了武術形式的專家的自然風格進行戰鬥，這是在實際行動中實現的最高成就。

你就是詠春#

Here at this level there is no Wing Chun, you are Wing Chun. You fight without thought for correct or incorrect technique; your response is automatic and instinctive.

在這個境界中，已經沒有所謂的詠春，你就是詠春。你戰鬥時不會去思考技術正確與否；你的反應是自動且出於本能的。

Exhibiting an icy calm and a seamless combination of key attributes (called jun/accuracy, wan/stability, fai/speed and geng/power) that comes from years of experience, skills are demonstrated that are the stuff of legends.

展現出冰冷的平靜與無縫結合的關鍵屬性：

特質Quality說明
準 （Jun）Accuracy精準
穩 （Wan）Stability穩定
快 （Fai）Speed速度
勁 （Geng）Power力量

這些來自多年的經驗，所展示的技能是傳奇的素材。

詠春的遺產#

This was the legacy left by the founders of Wing Chun Kung Fu, nothing less than true martial arts mastery. The road to finding that legacy begins with Qi Sao, the soul of Wing Chun.

這正是詠春拳創立者們所留下的遺產，也是真正的武術大師境界。而尋找這份遺產的道路，就始於詠春的靈魂——黐手。
